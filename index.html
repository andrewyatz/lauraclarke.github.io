#Laura Clarke's github page This page just gives you some information about Laura Clarke. ##Biography After studying Biochemistry and Genetics at Nottingham University and Bioinformatics at the University of Leeds, Laura started working in Bioinformatics in 2001 for the [Ensembl project](http://www.ensembl.org). In Ensembl Laura was a part of the gene annotation team and ran the gene annotation for Rat, Rhesus Macque and the Platypus, as well as helping to improve the teams pipeline infrastructure. In 2008 Laura moved to [EMBL-EBI](http://www.ebi.ac.uk) to run the data coordination centre for [the 1000 genomes project](http://www.1000genomes.org). At [EMBL-EBI](http://www.ebi.ac.uk). Laura lead's a small team who carry out data coordination and run primary analysis and QC pipelines for high throughput omics projects. The projects her team works on include: * [The 1000 genomes project](http://www.1000genomes.org). * [Blueprint](http://www.blueprint-epigenome.eu/) * [IHEC](http://ihec-epigenomes.org/) * [HipSci](http://www.hipsci.org) * [EBiSC](http://www.ebisc.org/) * [NextGen](http://projects.ensembl.org/nextgen/) She is a member of two git organisations and a contributor to many different git repos * [EMBL-EBI-GCA](https://github.com/EMBL-EBI-GCA), This is her team's repo collection. * [IHEC](https://github.com/IHEC), This is the organisation which represents code created by the different IHEC working groups.
